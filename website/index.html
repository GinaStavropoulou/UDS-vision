Urban Data Science

Our Mission

We develop open spatial tools, generate knowledge, and train professionals to create fairer, healthier and more resilient cities.

  • Measure and interpret the built environment through data
  • Open-source tools and reproducible research
  • Ethical, practice-ready urban data solutions

Our Pillars

Data Thinking

Approach the built environment as a complex phenomenon to be read through data, informing responsive urban design.

Impactful Education

Mentor and train professionals with technical excellence, ethics and data governance in mind.

Tools & Workflows

Open-source software, scalable workflows and 3D data solutions for practitioners and researchers.

Open Collaboration

Foster interdisciplinary, transparent and reproducible research grounded on open science principles.

Ethical GeoData

Commit to fairness, accountability and trustworthy data governance in methods and models.

Achievements

CityJSON — simple 3D city data

Urban Data Science is a creator and maintainer of CityJSON: a compact, web-friendly encoding for 3D city models used for visualization, analysis and exchange.
